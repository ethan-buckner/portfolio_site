Welcome!

PillagerCalc is a program that finds lethal combos for Pillager Rogue, a deck archetype for the Wild format of Hearthstone.

When given the set of cards in your hand, PillagerCalc will attempt to find a lethal combo. If it no lethal combo can be made with the current amount of mana, PillagerCalc will find the minimum amount of mana required to pull off a lethal combo with the cards you have in hand.

PillagerCalc currently supports the following cards: Spectral Pillager, Spirit of the Shark, Scabbs Cutterbutter, Foxy Fraud, Mailbox Dancer, Tenwu of the Red Smoke, Potion of Illusion, Shadowstep, Preparation, and the Coin.
